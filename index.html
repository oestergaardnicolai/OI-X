WHO IS TRYG?

  • Tryg is one of the largest insurance companies
    in the Nordic region with activities in Denmark, Norway and Sweden
  • Health is a strategic focus area in Tryg
  • Around 1,9 mio. people has a health insurance (2016)
  • Usually the health insurance is distributed through
    employers or pensions fund
  • Today Tryg has a market share of 15 % within health

Purpose

In a changing world, we make it easier to be tryg

In the coming strategy period 2018-2020 – we will support our purpose in the following ways:

Grasping opportunities to develop rather than defend our business i.e.:

  • Digitalisation
  • New products
  • Analytics

Adjusting to customer preferences and needs i.e.:

  • Self-service
  • Straight through processes
  • Packaging of products

Staying relevant to broaden our relevance in our customer´s lives i.e.:

  • Product innovation
  • Prevention
  • Add-on services

Show us how ‘we share my care’ with a collective funding opportunities for the elderly (year 67+) leaving the labor market, where the social contract of the group of peers or crowd keep focus on health and well-being throughout life using:

  • innovative software and technologies
  • support peer to peer, crowdfunding businessmodel, and
  • which attracts and repeatedly maintain a healthy lifestyle using the persistent interest of health we see in the of social media and communities
